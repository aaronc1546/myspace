Aaron's Blurbs

About me:

Started in marketing, got obsessed with building things, and ended up at Superhuman working on AI-native productivity. The throughline: figuring out what makes people tick and then making their tools match.

I've been to 6 continents. Antarctica is next. I've lived in Portland, San Francisco, and Austin — currently in Austin but thinking about returning to San Francisco later this year. I'm the kind of person who keeps a personal productivity OS in a git repo and thinks that's normal.

I like hard problems, clean systems, and dogs that are slightly too large for the couch.

Who I'd like to meet:

People building personal productivity and operating systems in Claude Code. Barack Obama. Richard Feynman. Damian Lillard. Anyone who's been to Antarctica.
